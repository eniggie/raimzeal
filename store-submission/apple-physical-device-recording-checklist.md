# Apple Guideline 1.2 Recording Checklist

Record on a physical iPhone or iPad using the replacement release build.

1. Delete any previous RAIMZEAL installation.
2. Install the replacement TestFlight build.
3. Open RAIMZEAL and navigate to Sign In.
4. Show that Sign In and Sign in with Apple require acceptance of Terms of Use and Community Guidelines.
5. Open the linked Terms and briefly show the Community Standards section.
6. Accept the agreement and sign in with the App Review demo account.
7. Open Community.
8. Tap `Safety` on another user's post.
9. Choose `Report objectionable content` and show the confirmation that the post is hidden and queued for review.
10. On another post, tap `Safety`, choose `Block abusive user`, and show the confirmation and feed removal.
11. End the recording after returning to the Community feed.

Upload the recording to App Review Information Notes before resubmitting.
